InternX is a fullstack web-based platform that connects high school students to employers (Linkedin for high school students). We were founded in BETA Camp's startup incubator summer 2020 cohort.

Users are able to login either as an employer or as a student.

Students can browse and apply to internships and positions at corporations, save and submit resumes, and favourite jobs.

Employers are able to login thorugh an employer account to post internships and opportunities at their companies and manage applicants for openings.
